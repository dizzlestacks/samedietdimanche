## Full Description

YARDEES is the ultimate marketplace for yard sale lovers and thrift shoppers. Whether you're hunting for vintage treasures, everyday deals, or looking to sell your pre-loved items, YARDEES connects you with your local community.

**Browse & Discover**
- Explore thousands of listings from yard sales, individual sellers, and thrift shops
- Filter by category, price, condition, and location
- Interactive map view to find sales happening near you
- Save your favorite items and get notified about new finds

**Sell with Ease**
- List items in minutes with up to 20 photos per listing
- Create yard sale events with dates, times, and locations
- Boost your listings to reach more buyers
- Track your sales with detailed analytics

**Connect & Communicate**
- Message buyers and sellers directly in the app
- Make offers and negotiate prices
- RSVP to upcoming yard sale events
- Read seller reviews and ratings

**Safe & Trusted**
- Verified buyer and seller profiles
- Content moderation for a clean marketplace
- Report suspicious listings
- Secure messaging system

**Features**
- Dark mode for comfortable browsing
- Available in 6 languages (English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Chinese)
- Works offline with cached listings
- Pull-to-refresh for latest deals
- Push notifications for messages and offers

Download YARDEES today and start discovering hidden treasures in your neighborhood!
